# Food Delivery Analysis

## Overview

Food delivery services have become an essential part of modern lifestyles. Understanding customer ordering behavior, restaurant performance, delivery efficiency, ratings, discounts, and revenue generation can help food delivery platforms improve customer satisfaction and optimize business operations.

This project performs an in-depth analysis of food delivery data using Python. Various factors such as order status, customer ratings, restaurant performance, delivery distance, preparation time, rider wait time, and discounts are analyzed to generate meaningful business insights.

---

## Problem Statement

Food delivery platforms handle thousands of orders daily. Analyzing customer behavior, delivery performance, and restaurant operations can help improve service quality, customer retention, and overall business growth.

The objective of this project is to analyze food delivery data and identify key factors that influence customer satisfaction, delivery efficiency, and revenue generation.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze food delivery order patterns.
- Identify top-performing restaurants.
- Study customer rating behavior.
- Analyze delivery distance patterns.
- Evaluate delivery efficiency.
- Investigate cancellation and rejection reasons.
- Generate actionable business insights.

## Key Findings

- Most orders were successfully delivered.
- Aura Pizzas received the highest number of customer orders.
- Customer ratings were generally positive.
- 5-star ratings were the most common among rated orders.
- Most customers preferred ordering from restaurants within 1–6 km.
- Kitchen Preparation Time and Rider Wait Time were maintained at reasonable levels.
- Promotional discounts influenced customer purchasing behavior.

---

## Business Insights

- Most orders were successfully delivered, indicating efficient delivery operations.
- Aura Pizzas emerged as the most popular restaurant based on order volume.
- Customers generally expressed satisfaction through positive ratings.
- Nearby restaurants received the majority of customer orders.
- Delivery efficiency was supported by acceptable preparation and waiting times.
- Order cancellations and rejections were relatively low.
- Discounts and promotional offers contributed significantly to customer engagement and revenue generation.

---

## Conclusion

This project successfully identified important patterns in customer behavior, restaurant performance, delivery efficiency, and revenue generation. The analysis revealed that customer satisfaction, restaurant popularity, delivery distance, operational efficiency, and promotional strategies significantly influence food delivery performance.

The insights derived from this analysis can help food delivery platforms improve service quality, enhance customer satisfaction, optimize delivery operations, and support data-driven business decisions.
